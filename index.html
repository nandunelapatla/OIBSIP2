A Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He was known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He was widely loved and admired for his humility and dedication to education and youth empowerment.

Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam rose from humble beginnings to become a national hero and a symbol of dedication to the nation.

He inspired millions through his speeches, books, and actions. His legacy continues to motivate generations of Indians.
